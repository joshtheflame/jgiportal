Navigate to HR Services ‚Üí Leave Management. Select your leave type, dates, and submit for approval. Your manager will be notified automatically.

Policy templates are available in the Document Repository under the Policies department. You can also use the search function to find specific templates.
How do I reset my password?
Contact IT Services at ext. 4500 or email [email protected]. For urgent issues, use the IT Support widget for live chat.

Payslips are uploaded by the 25th of each month. Access them via HR Services ‚Üí Payslips & Tax Forms.
